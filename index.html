AIOps Root Cause Analysis (Patent Pending)

The Problem: Modern systems generate tons of telemetry data — metrics (CPU, memory), logs (errors, events), and traces (request flows). Traditional tools look at these separately, which leads to noisy, siloed alerts and "alert fatigue," making it hard to spot the real issue.

The Solution: This patent introduces an AI-powered system that fuses all these signals into a unified structure, builds a cause-and-effect graph of events, and then works backward to pinpoint the true root cause of an incident (instead of just the noisy symptoms). What makes it stand out is a learning loop -- every time an SRE validates the system's prediction, it learns and adapts, becoming smarter with each incident.

The Innovation: In essence, this creates an AI Ops brain that doesn't just correlate data, but truly understands causation, and improves continuously like a human expert.
